ABOUT THE QUENCH HUNGER FOOD APP

 

The Quench Hunger Food App is an online based Mobile App designed to help customers locate your restaurant/ hotel, select foods they want to eat, place orders, make reservations, make payments and get their orders delivered to them using Dispatch riders. The Application is designed to manage day to day restaurant data and help create accuracy in order management and deliveries. It also helps customers in tracking foods delivery in real time from your restaurant to them(customers).

 

 

OUR PROCESS

 

  • We work with clients to understand their business and merge their concept with our already thought out functions to create a Standard App to suit their business.

KEY FUNCTIONALITIES

 

  1. Menu

 

  • List of menu categories. The menu will be grouped into categories, each category has a list of dishes.

 

  • List of products from category. After selecting a menu category, users can see the dishes or products, their prices and can add them to his/her orders or cart.

 

 

  1. Orders

 

  • Add order from menu. Users can make an order when he/she is outside the restaurant and come to the restaurant location to take the order or request a delivery.

 

  • Current order. Everything the users select from the menu will be visible in the current order, before placing it.

 

  1. Personalized Service

 

  1. Select current user. This allows several employees to access the interface by inputting their user code/password.

 

  1. See list of tables. The restaurant employees will see the list of tables with their status (reserved or Open) and will be able to open the table details screen.

 

  • Current order. The table details contain the current order with all its products

 

  • Change table or order. It helps customer to change their table for different reasons.

 

  • Invoice and partial invoice. It helps employees to print the partial invoice.

 

  • Make reservation. It helps employees to make and confirm reservation.

 

  • See list of orders outside the app. It helps to view list of all the reservations that are pending or active.

 

 

  1. Customer Sign up and Login

 

  • Customers can sign up and have accounts where they can login to place orders, add their details, write reviews, participate in promos and other features.

 

  1. Payments

 

This icon will contain all payment options which will be highly secured to protect customers details such as card and bank details.

 

TECHNICAL DESIGN

 

Design

 

  1. App icon, logo & splash screen.

 

  1. Mobile app design for smartphones (1242 x 2208) should work for smaller size screens with similar ratio and also will be compatible with different versions of Operating systems (Android, Blackberry and IOS).

 

  1. Restaurant web-based interface. This will be designed to run on touch screen PCs that have a printer connected.

 

  1. Service presentation website.

 

Mobile App

 

  1. This is meant to detect whether a user is inside or outside the restaurant/hotel. If the user is inside the restaurant, she/he will have small new features like partial payment or pinging the waiter.

 

  • Search if the restaurant has beacon technology and choose to enter.

 

  • Search using GPS to locate where the restaurant is located.

 

  • Request a table and wait for restaurant’s approval in cases where it is the restaurant’s setting.

 

  • Restaurant admins have a setting that controls when users can access your restaurant/hotels directly or if they need employee approval to access the restaurant.

 

  • Employees would wear a smartwatch to receive notifications in real time, instead of having to check the restaurant’s web interface.
